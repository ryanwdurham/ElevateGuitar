FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Everything you want to know before booking your first lesson.

Do I need any experience to start?
+

Absolutely not. Complete beginners are welcome. We start from scratch and build your skills from the ground up. All you need is a guitar and the desire to learn.

What age groups do you teach?
+

I teach students of all ages. Whether you're 10 or 60, it's never too early or too late to start. Lessons are tailored to each student's age and learning style.

Do you teach acoustic or electric guitar?
+

Both! Whether you want to play acoustic by the campfire or plug in and rock, I can teach you. We can also help you decide which is better for your goals.

Are lessons in-person or online?
+

I offer in-person lessons in Bothell, WA, and can also travel to you. Reach out to discuss what works best for your schedule and location.

What guitar should I buy as a beginner?
+

Great question — and it depends on the style you want to learn. Contact me before buying and I'll point you toward the right instrument for your budget and goals. No need to overspend at the start.

How often should I practice?
+

Consistency matters more than duration. Even 15–20 minutes of focused practice daily will produce faster results than an occasional 2-hour session. I'll build a realistic routine that works for your life.

Can I learn my favorite songs?
+

Yes — that's the whole point. Learning songs you love keeps you motivated and makes practice fun. We'll mix technical development with music you actually want to play.

How long are lessons?
+

I offer 30-minute and 60-minute lessons. Beginners often start with 30-minute sessions while more advanced students benefit from the full hour. We can discuss what's right for you.

How quickly will I see improvement?
+

Most students notice real improvement within the first few lessons. You'll leave your first session already able to play something. Consistent practice with proper guidance accelerates progress dramatically.

What styles and genres do you teach?
+

Rock, blues, metal, pop, alternative, and more. With 30+ years of experience across multiple genres and bands, I can teach you the style that excites you most.
